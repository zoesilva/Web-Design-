Meet the minds

Meet Marcelo Silva, the visionary behind Mercado. Born and raised in Portugal, Marcelo's passion for food and culinary traditions runs deep in his veins. From a young age, he found himself captivated by the bustling energy and vibrant flavors of the local Mercado (The Market), where he would often accompany his family on their weekly visits.

Drawing inspiration from his upbringing and a desire to share the authentic tastes of Portugal with the world, Marcelo embarked on a culinary journey that would eventually lead him to Montclair. Alongside his wife and partner, Carla, Marcelo set out to recreate the magic of his beloved Mercado in a modern setting that pays homage to tradition while embracing innovation.

With a background in hospitality and a keen eye for detail, Marcelo oversees every aspect of Mercado, from curating the finest ingredients to crafting inspired dishes. His commitment to quality, authenticity, and community shines through in every plate, reflecting his unwavering dedication to sharing the flavors of Portugal with the world.

Driven by a passion for excellence and a love for his homeland, Marcelo Silva is not a chef or a restaurant owner - he's a storyteller, a curator of memories, and a true ambassador of Portuguese cuisine. Through Mercado, he invites you to embark on a culinary adventure that celebrates the rich tapestry of flavors, traditions, and cultural heritage that define Portugal's culinary landscape.

Our food philosophy

Mercado is a celebration of the vibrant culture and rich culinary heritage found in traditional Portuguese marketplaces. Drawing inspiration from our roots and the bustling energy of Mercado (The Market) back in Portugal, we invite you on a journey filled with genuine ingredients, real food, and the heartwarming flavors of home.

Like the market vendor, we make the best of what we have and don't interfere with it too much. We keep food as natural as possible, deliberately avoiding complicated cooking methods. Unfussiness and simplicity in food preparation are, for us, the only way to maintain the freshness of a dish.

At Mercado, we pay homage to the cherished memories of our local food hall in Portugal, where butcher shops, charcuterie, fresh fish stands, and produce vendors come together to create a lively tapestry of tastes and aromas. It's where friends gather for a quick meal, a cup of coffee, or perhaps even a refreshing drink amidst the bustling ambiance. Join us as we share our love for genuine ingredients, real food, and the vibrant spirit of Mercado.
