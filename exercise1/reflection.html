Class Reflection

Reflecting on this web design class, I've come to appreciate the skills we've covered and how they can contribute to my growth as a creative individual. I initially saw web design as a technical skill, but now I understand its artistic aspects and how it blends technology with visual culture.

I'm mostly satisfied with the work I produced during the course. While there were definitely challenges, I feel like I've grown significantly in my ability to create visually appealing and functional websites. However, there's always room for improvement, and I'm excited to continue honing my skills.

This class has definitely changed my views on art, technology, and visual culture. I used to see them as separate entities, but now I understand how they intersect and influence each other. Website design is art, after all.

As for the future, I do plan on using my web design skills. Whether it's for personal projects or professional endeavors, I believe these skills will be valuable in a variety of contexts. However, I also recognize that coding websites may not be my primary focus.
